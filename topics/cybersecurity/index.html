Cybersecurity Terms

The “Cybersecurity Terms” category is a collection of terms and concepts related to the field of cybersecurity. It includes definitions and explanations of technical terms, such as malware, phishing, and encryption, as well as broader concepts like risk management and network security.

This category is designed to help individuals and organizations understand the various threats and vulnerabilities they may face in the digital world and to provide the knowledge and tools necessary to protect against these threats. It is a valuable resource for anyone interested in learning about cybersecurity, whether they are a beginner or an experienced professional.
