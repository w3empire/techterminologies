Hardware Terms

Hardware terms refer to the physical components of a computer system or device. This category includes a wide range of components, such as processors, memory, storage devices, input/output devices, and more.

These hardware elements are essential for the proper functioning of a computer system and are responsible for the processing, storing, and transferring of data. Understanding hardware terms is important for anyone working with or troubleshooting computer systems, as it allows them to identify and address issues related to specific hardware parts.
