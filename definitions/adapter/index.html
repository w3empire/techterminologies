What does 'Adapter' mean?

author Marvin Kaufman

An adapter is a device that allows two different systems or devices to connect and work together. It can be used to connect devices that have incompatible interfaces, or to add a new feature or function to a device.

There are many different types of adapters, including electrical adapters, audio and video adapters, network adapters, and computer adapters.

For example, an electrical adapter allows you to plug a device into an outlet with a different type of plug than the one on the device. An audio and video adapter allows you to connect a device with a different type of audio or video output to a TV or display with a different type of input. A network adapter allows you to connect a device to a network, such as a wired or wireless Ethernet connection. A computer adapter allows you to connect a device, such as a printer or external hard drive, to a computer.

Here are some more examples of adapters and how they are used:

  • A power adapter allows you to plug a device into an electrical outlet that has a different type of plug than the one on the device. For example, you might use a power adapter to plug a device with a European plug into an American outlet, or vice versa.
  • An HDMI adapter allows you to connect a device with a different type of video output, such as a laptop or smartphone, to a TV or display with an HDMI input.
  • A USB adapter allows you to connect a device with a different type of port, such as a printer or external hard drive, to a computer with a USB port.
  • A wireless adapter allows you to connect a device to a wireless network, such as a Wi-Fi router.
  • A memory card adapter allows you to use a memory card, such as a microSD card, in a device that has a different type of memory card slot, such as a SD card slot.
  • An adapter for a microphone allows you to use a microphone with a different type of connector, such as a 3.5mm jack, with a device that has a different type of input, such as a USB port.
  • An adapter for a camera allows you to use a camera lens with a different type of mount on a camera body with a different type of mount.

Adapters are useful because they allow you to use a device in a different environment or with different types of equipment. They can also be used to add new features or functions to a device, such as the ability to connect to a network or to use a different type of input or output.
